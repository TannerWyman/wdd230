Main Target Audience Design

Target Audience

Local people looking to see what else their town has to offer as well as people looking to promote their buisness. Most people will be in the age group of about 25 to 50.

Personas

Persona A: Stanley Smith

Stanley is a local business owner who runs a bakery. He hasn’t owned his bakery for long and is still new to being an entrepreneur. He wants to promote his bakery, but he hasn’t had much luck so far with newspaper ads and fliers.

Persona B: Lisa Jenson

Lisa is a mother of 2 children under the age of 10. She enjoys spending time outside and likes to take her kids with her. To keep things interesting she likes to vist new and diffrent places. Some times she plans these trips in advance and needs to know the weather for those days.

Scenarios

  • What is the local weather?
  • How can I join the local Chamber of Commerce? What fees are there?
  • What neat things are there around the town?
  • What kinds of local businesses are there?
  • When is the next local event?
  • How can I contact the local Chamber of Commerce?
